Real Estate in Mexico City

April 2021


According to Mercado Libre and Inmuebles 24

If you were to look for a property to buy in Mexico City and you searched in Mercado Libre or Inmuebles 24's website, this is what you will find.


The following project was completed by analyzing 4,014 ads on Mercado Libre and Inmuebles 24 when looking for properties to buy in Mexico City.


*Note: Unfortunately after cleaning the data for this project, it couldn't be added "Milpa Alta" District, as the number of results were very few, they had inconsistencies, skewed the data or seemed suspcious at the time of withdrawing the information.*


**Maps on this website are seen best on a desktop screen**



What can we get from the maps above?





1st Key Insight - Perhaps you knew it already...



The most expensive districts to buy Real Estate:

  • Miguel Hidalgo
  • Cuajimalpa de Morelos
  • Álvaro Obregón
  • Cuauhtémoc
  • Benito Juárez

The 5 LEAST expensive districts to buy Real Estate:

  • Tláhuac
  • Xochimilco
  • Iztapalapa
  • Venustiano Carranza
  • Azcapotzalco


2nd Key Insight - Iztapala


Theorically speaking, with more population, more scarce Real Estate is and as a result, more expensive is to buy a property. But this is wrong, In Mexico City, "Iztapala" is the district with the highest number of people in Mexico City but the Real Estate there is some of the cheapest in the city. Perhaps insecurity, lack of public services in part of the district and almost none big corporative districts are the reason for this.





3rd key insight - Less people, more space, but more expensive per m2?




In other countries, leaving the city to the suburbs or somewhere far from the City Center is a decision in which you expect to increase your living space and find cheaper deals for Real Estate, in Mexico city there are some districts that eventhough are some of the least populated in the city and the size of properties are some of the biggest, the price per m2 are the most expensive of all districts. These districts we are talking about are: "Cuajimalpa de Morelos", "Miguel Hidalgo" and "Benito Juárez". Perhaps being closer to business districts has advantages to them.



4th key insight - Looking for bargains of Real Estate online?


If you are looking to buy a property in Mexico City, but you want to search for the Real Estate bargains, online might not be the solution for you, as many of the properties advertised on Mercado Libre and Inmuebles 24 are located on the most expensive districts of the city. So if you are confident and love strolls, perhaps looking for deals on a more traditional way will be a better choice for you
